1. 
The present petition under Article 227 of the Constitution of India impugns the
judgment dated 04th August, 2018 passed by the Additional District Judge - 05, South-
East, Saket Courts, New Delhi in Suit No. 1462/2017, whereby the application filed on
behalf of the respondent/defendant under Section 8 of the Arbitration and Conciliation
Act, 1996 (hereinafter referred to as 'Act') has been allowed.
2. 
This matter came up for hearing before this Court on 11th December, 2018 when
notice was issued. Subsequently an application for early hearing was filed on behalf of
the petitioner which was allowed by this Court on 9th September, 2021. The respondent
has filed a reply and the petitioner has also filed a rejoinder.
